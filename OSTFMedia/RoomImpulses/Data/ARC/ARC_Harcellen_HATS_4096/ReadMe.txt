Modified by Erik Witte, 2024
The following modifications were made to each file:
- The initial 1080 samples were removed
- Final samples were removed so that the remaining sounds had a total length of 4096 samples.
- Linear fade in during the first 20 samples were applied.
- Linear fade out during the last 500 samples were applied.
